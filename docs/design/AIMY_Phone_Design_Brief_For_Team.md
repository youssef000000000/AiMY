## Brief

Hi team / [Manager name],

Please find attached the first set of **AiMY Phone** mobile UI/UX screens. These mockups align with the Jira spec (in-app calling for AiMY Talent and AiMY Sales, Twilio Voice, mobile-first).

**What you’re seeing**

- **User journey flow** — Diagram of the two main flows (outbound and incoming).
- **Incoming call** — Full-screen takeover with caller ID, context card, and Answer / Decline / Remind me.
- **Active call** — Live transcript, AI nudges, and mute / hold / end controls.
- **Mini-player** — Floating bar when the rep navigates away during a call (Return to call).
- **Post-call** — AI summary, insights, action cards, and save to profile.
- **Profile (tap-to-call)** — Candidate/lead profile with a clear Call button when a number exists.

**Design approach**

- Dark theme consistent with AiMY (background #0D1117, accents blue/purple/green).
- Base frame 390×844 (iPhone 14); touch targets and spacing follow the design spec.
- Flows are ordered by user journey (outbound: Profile → Active call → Mini-player → Post-call; incoming: Incoming call → Active call → Mini-player → Post-call).

**Ask**

I’d like your **opinions and feedback** on:

1. **Layout and hierarchy** — Is the most important information easy to see and use?
2. **Incoming call** — Does the context card + three actions (Answer / Decline / Remind me) work for you?
3. **Active call** — Is the balance between transcript, nudges, and controls clear?
4. **Post-call** — Are the summary, insights, and action cards in the right order and density?
5. **Anything missing or confusing** — What would you change or add?

Please reply with your thoughts when you have a moment. I’ll consolidate feedback and update the designs accordingly.
